About Me


I am Indra Prasad P., a passionate sports enthusiast with a deep love for physical activities and games that bring out the best in people. Sports have always been more than just a hobby for me; they are a way of life. My journey in sports has taught me the values of teamwork, perseverance, and leadership.


Over the years, I have participated in various sports and achieved milestones that I'm proud of. These include being a district champion in Kabaddi, excelling in track and field events like Javelin Throw and Shot Put, and leading my cricket team to victory in a local league championship. Each of these achievements represents hard work, dedication, and the thrill of competition.


Sports Achievements

  • District Champion in Kabaddi (2018)
  • Runner-up in Shot Put and Javelin Throw (2018)
  • Winner of Local Cricket League (2020)

Beyond my achievements, I am also deeply committed to mentoring and guiding aspiring athletes. I believe in sharing my knowledge and helping others discover their potential. Whether it's offering tips on technique, strategy, or fitness, I enjoy being a resource for others who share my love for sports.


When I'm not on the field or court, I enjoy learning about the history of various sports, staying updated with the latest trends, and exploring ways to improve my skills. Sports have shaped my personality, making me a more disciplined, focused, and resilient individual.
